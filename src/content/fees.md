### 2025/26 Season Registration Fee: $330

#### What Is Included

- US Club Soccer player and coach membership fee
- City field access
- NorCal fall and spring league home game referee fees
- Club equipment
- Registration transaction and admin fees

### Payment Options

- **Pay in Full:** One-time payment of $330 at registration
- **Payment Plan:**
  - $80 due at registration
  - $50 due July 1
  - $50 due August 1
  - $50 due September 1
  - $50 due October 1
  - $50 due November 1

### Team Fee

In addition to club registration, each team typically collects a team fee between $350 and $550 per year to cover expenses such as league entries, State Cup referees, tournaments, team equipment, and other team-specific costs.

### Uniform

- Uniform kit is purchased separately and is not included in registration fee
- Estimated cost: $120 plus shipping
- Typical kit includes three jerseys, two shorts, and two pairs of socks
