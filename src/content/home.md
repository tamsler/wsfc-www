## Program Snapshot

West Sacramento Soccer Club Competitive Program supports long-term player development with age-appropriate training, experienced coaching, and a strong team-first culture.

### Why Families Choose WSFC

- Competitive pathway from U09 through U19
- Local training environment with strong community roots
- Focus on technical growth, game understanding, and character
- Clear communication around tryouts, fees, and season expectations

### 2026 Tryout Windows

- May 12 - 14, 2026: Girls and Boys U09 - U14
- May 19 - 21, 2026: Girls and Boys U15 - U19
